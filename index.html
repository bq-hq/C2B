About Us

At blue queue, we are a passionate and committed group of people who see an imbalance between us as consumers and big tech + the brands they represent. Every brand has Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software that helps them market and sell to us. What do we have? A million apps, our data is spread out everywhere, and no common way to relate to them on our terms.

This is why were building blue queue, so that consumers like us can relate to brands in an organized way, own our own data, and be rewarded for it! We believe blue queue should be designed from your perspective, from pre-purchase to the end-of-life of a product, giving us tools for every life event. All of life's lists and a personal AI assistant for every step along the way.

Join us to help balance how we relate as Consumers to Brands - we call this C2B.
